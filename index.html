Welcome! Are you developer exploring for a smooth name for your personal website and projects? You came to the right place!

thedev.id is a free identity for developers on the web. This service offers a handy subdomain service for you to have a subdomain like foo.thedev.id or bar.thedev.id for use on your static sites via CNAME. Fork, add subdomain, and send a Pull Request. We will make it work.
